# civic-data-transcriptions

AI-generated transcriptions of publicly available civic meeting recordings.

## Disclaimer

**THIS REPOSITORY IS PROVIDED FOR PERSONAL, INFORMATIONAL USE ONLY.**

All transcriptions in this repository were generated by automated AI speech-to-text systems (ElevenLabs Scribe v2) and **are not official records**. They have not been reviewed, verified, or approved by any government body, elected official, or authorized clerk.

**These transcriptions almost certainly contain errors**, including but not limited to:
- Misattributed speaker identifications
- Incorrectly transcribed words, names, numbers, and legal references
- Missing or garbled sections of audio
- Inaccurate timestamps

**Do not rely on these transcriptions for any legal, official, journalistic, or decision-making purpose.** Always refer to the official records, minutes, and recordings published by the relevant government body.
